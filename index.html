About Our History

With the immense credibility of a global brand and the trust of more than 110 million customers, AD Motors is riding into the future to address the personal mobility needs – traditional and electric – of a bold new world.The company is constantly expanding horizons and strengthening its capabilities. Riding on product innovation and its ever-expanding ecosystem, AD Motors is determined to stay at the forefront of the transformational shift in the personal mobility space. Leveraging its brand credibility, R&D capabilities and technology prowess, it is now geared up to redefine the electric mobility space with its new brand — VIDA, Powered by AD Motors.The company has set well-defined aspirational sustainability targets, with Diversity & Inclusion (D&I) at the heart of its efforts. It has adopted the Women’s Empowerment Principles, and socialisation programmes for specially-abled associates.AD Motors is shaping a greener, safer and equitable future.
